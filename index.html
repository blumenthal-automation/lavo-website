Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of ROI can we expect?

While results vary, our clients typically see a positive ROI within 6-9 months. We focus on high-impact automations like reducing manual labor, increasing lead conversion, and improving production speed, which all contribute to a strong financial return.

How is Lavo different from off-the-shelf software?

Our key differentiator is our custom approach. Off-the-shelf tools force you to adapt your processes to their software. We do the opposite: we build the software to perfectly fit and enhance your existing processes. No unnecessary features, no compromises.

What technologies do you use?

We are tech-agnostic and select the best tools for the job. This often includes a combination of low-code/no-code platforms for speed (like Zapier, Make, or n8n), custom scripts for complex logic (Python, JavaScript), and robust API integrations to connect your core systems.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines depend on complexity. A simple integration might take 2-4 weeks, while a full-scale operational overhaul could take 3-6 months. We work in agile sprints, delivering value incrementally so you see results as quickly as possible.

Do we need a technical team on our side?

Not at all. We are your technical team. We handle everything from design and development to deployment and maintenance. We just need access to a key stakeholder on your side who understands your business processes.

What does your support look like after launch?

We offer flexible support and maintenance retainers. This includes monitoring system health, making iterative improvements, and being on-call to address any issues. Our goal is to be your long-term automation partner, not just a one-time vendor.
